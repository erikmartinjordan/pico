04 · Questions

Questions.

Is Orange Fuji free?

Orange Fuji has a 30-day trial. After the trial, buy a license and activate it with your checkout email.

How does the license work?

Each license allows 2 active device activations. Active licenses are revalidated online periodically, while captures and recordings remain local.

What platforms does Orange Fuji support?

macOS (Intel & Apple Silicon), Windows (portable), and Linux (AppImage). Same feature set on all three.

Does Orange Fuji upload my data?

No. Everything runs locally. No analytics, no telemetry, no accounts. Your screen stays yours.

How do I get started?

Download the binary for your platform, launch it, and press Cmd+Shift+S (macOS) or Ctrl+Shift+S. No install wizard needed.
