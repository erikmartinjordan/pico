Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

Built for QA engineers, support teams, product managers and anyone who documents with screenshots.

Instant capture

Grab the entire screen across all monitors with a single shortcut. The image loads immediately in the editor.

Annotation tools

Rectangles, arrows and text — with adjustable color and stroke width. Draw directly on the capture, no layers.

Zero install, portable

Single pico.exe — run it from a USB drive, shared folder or your desktop. No admin rights needed.

How it works

Three steps. Thirty seconds.

No setup wizards. No accounts. No cloud uploads.

Capture or open

Press Ctrl+Shift+S to capture your screen, or Ctrl+O to open an existing image.

Annotate

Select a tool — rectangle, arrow or text — pick a color and draw directly on the image.

Export & share

Hit Ctrl+E to save as PNG. Drag it into Slack, email or your bug tracker.
